Frequently Asked Questions
What is Nurture Farm Market?
Nurture Farm Market is an online platform that connects farmers and customers directly, allowing the purchase and sale of fresh agricultural products.
How do I register as a customer or farmer?
You can register through our website by clicking on the Register button and filling out the form with the required details.
How can I track my order?
Go to the 'My Orders' section from your profile dropdown to view current and past orders, along with tracking details.
Is product quality guaranteed?
Absolutely! We ensure that all listed products are fresh and of high quality. Our team and farmers follow strict standards to maintain quality from farm to doorstep.
What if I receive a damaged or spoiled product?
If you receive a damaged product, you can request a replacement or refund by contacting support within 24 hours of delivery. We prioritize customer satisfaction.
What features does the website offer for users?
Our platform offers an easy registration process, personalized user panel, order tracking, secure checkout, and the ability to connect directly with farmers.
How do farmers benefit from this platform?
Farmers can sell directly to customers without middlemen, ensuring better profit margins and exposure to a wider market.
How do I contact support?
Visit our Help section or use the Contact form at the bottom of our homepage to reach out to our support team.
